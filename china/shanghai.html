China: Shanghai

Landscapes

The Bund is a walking tour between Baidu Bridge and Jinling East Road outside the west bank of Huangpu in Shanghai. At night, the two sides of the Pujiang River are lit with neon lights, and it is suitable for strolling with your lover on the Bund, feeling the charm and romance of “Never Sleeping City”.

The Shanghai World Financial Center stands in the Lujiazui Finance and Trade Zone and is similar in shape to a bottle opener. Boarding the 100-story sightseeing hall, you can look directly at the peak of the Oriental Pearl Tower.

The Oriental Pearl Tower, 468 meters high, is located in Shanghai's Lujiazui Financial Quarter. It faces the Bund across the river and is one of Shanghai's landmark buildings.
