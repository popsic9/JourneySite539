Asia: China



Shanghai is one of the four municipalities under the direct administration of the central government of China, with a population of more than 24 million as of 2017. It’s a global financial center and transport hub. It’s also my home town. I have lived there for almost 22 years.

Beijing is China’s capital city. It was first established around 3,000 years ago and has historically been the capital of the nation for more than 800 years. This place has a long and storied history and culture.

Chengdu is a city on the rise. It has a unique cuisine with a huge number of hot and spicy dishes, as well as an enviously relaxed pace of life.
